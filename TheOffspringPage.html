The Offspring


My Thoughts

Though I've been listening to them for a long time now, I never actually got around to listening to almost only Offspring for an extended period of time. Furthermore, I only really focused on the two or three albums I knew for the longest time (being Ixnay on the Hombre and Smash). After I branched out into Americana and eventually Ignition, I found myself liking them more than I ever had. Some titles, as with Green Day, did eventually span more into "emo" music as a genre and strayed from the original sound of early Offspring, and I definitely disliked those songs and albums more. However, all in all they've been one of my favorite bands that I was actually lucky enough to see live a few years ago.
